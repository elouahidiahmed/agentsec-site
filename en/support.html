Help for our applications and games

A question, a bug, a suggestion? We're here to help.

Contact us

Reach us by email at contact@agentsec.ca. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

For faster handling

When you write about an issue, please include if possible:

  • the name of the app or game concerned;
  • your device model and Android version;
  • the app version (shown on the Google Play listing);
  • a description of the problem and, if possible, a screenshot;
  • the steps to reproduce the problem.
